Installing Yggdrasil

Download the Windows installer: yggdrasil-0.5.12-x64.msi

Run the installer and start Yggdrasil.

Configuring Yggdrasil

Edit %ALLUSERSPROFILE%\Yggdrasil\yggdrasil.conf and set our peers or choose your own:

  Peers: [
    tls://novaprospekt.holz.gg:9001
    tls://whiteforest.holz.gg:9001
  ]

Restart the Yggdrasil service from Services or restart your computer.

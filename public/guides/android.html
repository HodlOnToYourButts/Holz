Install Yggdrasil

Download the Android APK: app-release-unsigned.apk

Install the APK on your Android device.

Configure Yggdrasil

Open Yggdrasil and press the Peers button:

Press the + button then enter tls://novaprospekt.holz.gg:9001

Press the + button then enter tls://whiteforest.holz.gg:9001

Go to Settings ‚Üí Network & Internet ‚Üí VPN ‚Üí Yggdrasil:

Always-on VPN

Block connections without VPN

Install Alfis

Download the Android APK: alfis-android-v0.1.0-beta-release-unsigned.apk

Install the APK on your Android device.

Configure DNS

Open Yggdrasil and click on DNS Servers:

Press the + button then enter 127.0.0.1:5353

Testing the Setup

To confirm everything is working correctly, open your web browser and visit:

http://sanctum.ygg/

If the page loads successfully, your Yggdrasil and Alfis setup is working properly!

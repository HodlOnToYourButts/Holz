Installing Yggdrasil

Download the Android APK: app-release-unsigned.apk

Install the APK on your Android device.

Configuring Yggdrasil

Configure Yggdrasil to connect to our peers or choose your own:

Adding Peers

  1. Click the Peers button
  2. Press the + button to add a new peer
  3. Add: tls://novaprospekt.holz.gg:9001
  4. Press the + button again
  5. Add: tls://whiteforest.holz.gg:9001

Configuring DNS

  1. Click the DNS Servers button
  2. Press the + button
  3. Add: 127.0.0.1:5353

Installing Alfis

Download the Android APK: alfis-android-v0.1.0-beta-release-unsigned.apk

Install the APK on your Android device.

Testing the Setup

To confirm everything is working correctly, open your web browser and visit:

http://sanctum.ygg/

If the page loads successfully, your Yggdrasil and Alfis setup is working properly!
